The Central Florida Geeks Amateur Radio Society, is a small group of amateurs located in and around Central Florida.  The group was formed by amateurs whose interests are DX, VHF/UHF, Satellite, Packet, APRS & Linux. Thanks for checking in.  73 de the gang at CFGARS.

Our purpose: Fun, Fellowship and promoting Amateur Radio.

Meetings: As our schedules allow, we get together for breakfast, lunch, hamfest and other activities.

Projects: A Full service 2Mtrs. Repeater, IRLP and Weather Station.
