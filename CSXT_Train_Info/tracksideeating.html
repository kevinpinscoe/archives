Sykesville MD Baldwin's     Old B&O station designed by famed B&O architect E. Francis Baldwin. Built in 1884. One of only two "Baldwins" still standing on the Old Main Line, the other being in Ellicott City where one of the B&O museums now is.   Kevin Inscoe 7/5/2001 http://baldwinstation.com 7618 Main Street 410-795-1041
Asheville NC Biltmore Depot Restaurant NS Saluda Line Both are at the east end of Asheville yard, where US25 crosses the tracks in Biltmore, right where the NS lines to Black Mountain and points east and to Saluda grade and points south diverge. On the south side of the junction is the Depot Restaurant, located in the old station where the Vanderbilts used to detrain to go to their estate, the entrance to which is just west of the station. Train
views are somewhat restricted from this restaurant, right on the tracks, but still interesting place to eat, surrounded by lots of history. Often westbound trains stop at the home signal controlling entrance to the yard and the engines idle right in front of the station until cleared to enter yard. This station was in active
passenger service until Southern RR quit passenger trains in 1972. 		Fresh grilled seafood, veal, prime rib, burgers, salads, soups. Chicken,s teaks, sandwiches and pasta. Lunch and dinner 7 days a week. All major credit cards. Bob rlowry98@aol.com   http://www.biltmorevillage.com/biltmore6.htm 30 Lodge Street, Asheville NC 28803 828-277-7651
